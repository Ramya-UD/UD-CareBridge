About Our Mission

Connecting surplus food to orphaned children through technology

🌍 Who We Are

CareBridge is a social impact platform created by U D RAMYA to reduce food waste and support orphanages using IoT monitoring.

👩‍💻 Developer Contribution

The CareBridge platform is an IoT-based food safety and donation monitoring system.

Responsibilities include hardware integration, cloud connectivity, web dashboard development, and system testing.

❤️ Our Vision

A future where no child sleeps hungry and no good food is wasted.

🤝 Our Impact

Supporting orphanages across Andhra Pradesh using smart food safety monitoring.

📍 Focus Region

Dedicated to supporting orphaned children across Andhra Pradesh.

Project Development Timeline

Innovation Journey of CareBridge

From concept to real-time food safety monitoring platform

Concept & Research

🟢 2025 — Observed two major issues: food wastage and lack of safe food delivery to orphanages.

Hardware Integration

Integrated ESP32, MQ-135 gas sensor, and DHT22 temperature & humidity sensor.

Cloud Connectivity

Configured Wi-Fi communication and Firebase real-time database for monitoring.

Web Dashboard

Developed web interface to display food freshness status and alerts.

CareBridge Platform Launch

Connected food donors and orphanages with smart monitoring and safety validation.
